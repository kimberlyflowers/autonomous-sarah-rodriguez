Bloomie for real estate agents

The agent does not need another script. The agent needs someone working the seller list.

Chatbots tell agents how to prospect. You tell a Bloomie who to work. Expired listings, FSBOs, valuation leads, old seller leads, missed replies, open house contacts, and cold follow-up can become a scheduled daily role instead of another task the agent avoids after the third no.

Real estate agents are outbound salespeople. The hard part is not knowing what to do.

Agents know listings drive the business. They know they need seller leads, fast follow-up, local content, CRM discipline, and consistent outreach. The pain is rejection, inconsistency, and the mental tax of making the next call after the last person said no.

What a Bloomie can take off the agent

  • Daily expired listing, FSBO, valuation lead, and old seller lead follow-up
  • Approved AI voice, SMS, email, or CRM touches through connected tools
  • Seller qualification by timeline, motivation, property, agent status, and appointment readiness
  • CRM notes, pipeline stages, follow-up reminders, and no-interest tagging
  • Listing content, neighborhood updates, open house follow-up, and seller nurture campaigns

The "too good to be true" dream

Let your Bloomie take the hang-ups, brush-offs, and "not interested" replies, then pass you the promising seller leads worth your actual voice.

Sample 4-hour real estate Bloomie workday

This is not a chatbot giving advice. This is an assigned seller-prospecting work block built around the outcomes that can change an agent's month.

8-9 AMBuild and prioritize the seller list: expired listings, FSBOs, home valuation leads, old seller leads, open house contacts, and missed replies.
9-10 AMRun approved outreach through connected tools and follow up with yesterday's not-now and no-answer leads.
10-11 AMQualify positive replies by property, motivation, timeline, current agent relationship, and appointment readiness.
11-12 PMUpdate CRM, schedule next touches, draft seller nurture content, and hand the agent warm conversations or booked appointments.

Why not just use a chatbot?

A chatbot can write a cold call script or suggest a follow-up message. A Bloomie is assigned to actually work the list for you inside weekly managed scheduled tasks. The agent still controls client relationships, pricing strategy, listing appointments, negotiations, and final decisions.
