## Margin Risk Scenarios

| Scenario | Additional Cost | Margin Impact |
|---|---|---|
| Content creator (200 videos/month) | +$222 in video | Margin drops to ~$902 (75%) |
| Mass emailer (50,000 emails/month) | +$34 in email sends | Margin stays ~$1,090 (91%) |
| Heavy SMS user (5,000 texts/month) | +$48 in SMS | Margin stays ~$1,076 (90%) |
| All of the above combined | +$304 | Margin drops to ~$820 (68%) |

**Conclusion:** Even worst-case combined heavy usage maintains 68%+ margins. Video is the only meaningful cost driver, and it's gated behind Enterprise with a 30-video cap.
