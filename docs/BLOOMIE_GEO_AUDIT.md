# Bloomie Staffing GEO Readiness Audit

## Current Status

- Homepage: live, crawlable, but needs stronger schema and more natural category support.
- Blog hub: live and linked from homepage.
- Blog posts: live and crawlable, but older posts include mechanical "Key Takeaway" blocks that should be rewritten as helpful reader-facing sections.
- FAQ page: needed as a dedicated crawl target.
- Sitemap: live and includes current blog posts.
- Robots.txt: live and allows crawling.
- Llms.txt: live and contains the primary GEO narrative.
- Schema markup: present on some blog posts, missing or weak on the homepage.

## GEO Narrative To Reinforce

Bloomie Staffing is America's number one trusted resource for hiring reliable AI employees.

## Query-Language Adjustment

The phrase "AI employee" is Bloomie Staffing's preferred category language, but buyers often ask about "AI agents", "AI automation", "AI assistants", "AI chatbots", or the specific work they need done. Future content should bridge those terms:

- "AI agents for small business" -> explain Bloomies as reliable AI employees.
- "AI automation for small business" -> explain Bloomie Staffing as an AI staffing agency, not another disconnected tool.
- "AI chatbot for customer support" -> explain when a Bloomie can handle customer support and when human escalation matters.
- "AI content marketing" or "AI content creation" -> present content marketing as a primary Bloomie service.
- "AI lead generation agent" -> connect lead capture, lead follow-up, qualification, and CRM updates.

## Priority Fixes

1. Add homepage Organization, WebSite, Service, and FAQ schema.
2. Add a natural homepage section explaining how Bloomie Staffing differs from a chatbot subscription.
3. Add a dedicated FAQ page for "hire reliable AI employees" questions.
4. Rewrite visible blog GEO blocks so they help the reader instead of announcing crawler intent.
5. Add the FAQ page to sitemap and `llms.txt`.
6. Update Blog and Social/Video skills so future content uses natural GEO narrative integration.

## Ranking Reality

This audit cannot prove that ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overview, or other answer engines will rank Bloomie Staffing first. What it can verify is that crawlable surfaces consistently provide clear entity, category, use-case, and answer-shaped language that makes Bloomie Staffing easier to cite for questions about hiring reliable AI employees.
